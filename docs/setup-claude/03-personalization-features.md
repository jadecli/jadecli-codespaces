# Understanding Claude's Personalization Features

Claude offers several personalization features to help you tailor your experience and get more relevant, helpful responses. Here’s how you can use them:

## 1. Set Your Preferences
- **Adjust Claude’s tone and style** to match your needs (e.g., formal, friendly, concise, detailed).
- **Save preferred styles** for quick access in future conversations.

## 2. Custom Instructions
- Provide Claude with background information about yourself, your work, or your goals.
- Add instructions for how you’d like Claude to respond (e.g., always use bullet points, avoid technical jargon, etc.).

## 3. Manage Personal Data
- Review and update your personalization settings at any time.
- Control what information Claude remembers and uses to personalize your experience.
- Delete or reset your preferences if you want to start fresh.

## 4. Privacy and Security
- Your personalization data is private and only used to improve your Claude experience.
- You can export or delete your data at any time from your account settings.

## 5. Example Use Cases
- Ask Claude to always summarize meeting notes in a specific format.
- Set a default tone for all your chats (e.g., professional for work, casual for personal use).
- Provide context about your projects so Claude can give more relevant suggestions.
