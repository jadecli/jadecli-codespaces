# Using Claude App Intents, Shortcuts, and Widgets on iOS

Claude’s iOS app supports powerful features for quick access and automation. Here’s how to use intents, shortcuts, and widgets:

## 1. App Intents
- Use Siri or the Shortcuts app to trigger Claude actions with your voice or automations.
- Set up custom intents for common tasks (e.g., summarize a note, start a new chat).

## 2. Shortcuts
- Create shortcuts to quickly launch Claude with specific prompts or actions.
- Combine Claude with other apps in multi-step shortcuts for advanced workflows.

## 3. Widgets
- Add Claude widgets to your iOS home screen for instant access to recent chats or quick actions.
- Customize widget size and content to fit your workflow.

## 4. Getting Started
- Open the Claude app and go to settings to explore available intents and shortcuts.
- Add widgets by long-pressing the home screen and selecting Claude from the widget gallery.
