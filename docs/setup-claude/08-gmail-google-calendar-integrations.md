# Using the Gmail and Google Calendar Integrations

Claude can connect to your Gmail and Google Calendar accounts for smarter scheduling and communication. Here’s how to use these integrations:

## 1. Connecting Gmail and Calendar
- Go to Claude’s integrations or settings page.
- Select Gmail or Google Calendar and follow the prompts to connect your account.
- Grant the necessary permissions for email and calendar access.

## 2. Managing Emails and Events
- View, search, and send emails directly from Claude.
- Schedule, view, and manage calendar events within your chats or projects.
- Get reminders and suggestions based on your schedule.

## 3. Privacy and Security
- You control which data Claude can access.
- Revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.
- All data access is secure and private.
